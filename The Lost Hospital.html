Game Story

The Lost Hospital is a short horror experience that revolves around the story of a man named Josiah, He is the only child of Welhern who died due to the serious explosion of their house. Josiah was also in the house that exploded, but he was the only one who was rushed to the hospital.

His objective is to find out what caused their house to explode Will he succeed in his quest to prove his family that he has found the impossible-justice, or will he meet his demise at the hands of the demons that lie in wait? Only time will tell as Josiah's on a treacherous journey fraught with danger and uncertainty.
